Modules

Learning Objectives & Assignments

1 Employer Branding 2 Recruitment and Selection 3 Performance Management 4 Legal Aspects of HR 5 Financial Aspects of HR 6 Training and Development 7 Employee Engagement 8 HR Balanced Scorecard 9 HR Strategy
Employer Branding
Self-paced & online

Learn what is Employer Branding and how to build and execute an Employer Branding Strategy for a company of your choice.

Project:
Build an Employer Branding strategy.

Recruitment and Selection
Live in Classroom

Learn winning recruitment strategies, key steps in the recruitment and selection process such as job analysis, identification of skills gap, advertising, sourcing, screening, interviewing, and onboarding of candidates.

Project:
Develop a comprehensive recruitment plan for a specific job offering.

Performance Management
Live in Classroom

Understand the performance management process, develop performance objectives, provide effective feedback, conduct performance appraisals, and create a plan for reward-based performance.

Project:
Develop a comprehensive performance management plan for a specific job or department based on a real-life scenario.

Legal Aspects of HR
Live in Classroom

Learn about legal frameworks related to employment legislation and understand practical aspects of employment situations from beginning until termination.

Project:
Quiz assessment!

Financial Aspects of HR
Live in Classroom

Learn about data implementation and HR Finance, understand data management and report making. Identify opportunities and challenges, learn how to decide on pay structure and incentives.

Project:
Quiz assessment!

Training and Development
Live in Classroom

Understand the Competency Framework, assess Training necessities, learn HR Management and implement Learning and Development programs using training evaluation, employee development methods, and training facilitation.

Project:
Quiz assessment!

Employee Engagement
Live in Classroom

Measure NPS, learn techniques for increased productivity, company culture, solving critical issues amongst teams, and driving activities for team building and overall employee wellbeing.

Project:
Prepare an Employee survey and summarize the results.

HR Balanced Scorecard
Self-paced* English language

Learn the fundamentals of a balanced scorecard framework, focus on HR's perspective, and understand how to create an HR Scorecard for performance measurement and reporting.

Project:
Develop an HR Balanced Scorecard for your company.

HR Strategy
Self-paced* English language

Develop strategic thinking, understand how to align HR strategies with the overall business goals, develop an HR strategy based on the business requirements and challenges, and measure its effectiveness.

Project:
Develop an HR strategy for your company.
